Final Project

A Smart Kid Tiffin Box.

  • Design and Layout

    Plan the layout of the tiffin box, ensuring it has separate compartments for different types of food.

  • Mobile App Development

    a mobile app that parents and children can use to interact with the tiffin box. Include features like meal scheduling, reminders, and food tracking.

  • Partnerships for the Goals

    Collaborative efforts among parents, educators, and technology developers can drive the success of this project.

  • Zero Hunger

    While not directly eliminating hunger, the project contributes to reducing hunger by ensuring that children have access to meals. The project's impact on these goals depends on its implementation and the extent to which it addresses key issues related to health, education, and sustainability. Proper planning and execution can maximize its positive effects on the SDGs
